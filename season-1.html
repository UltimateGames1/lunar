Season 1

Season 1 will release . . .
 

Practice

  • All statistics will be reset.
  • Premium Queue will be implemented. At the end of the season, the player in 1st place will receive $100, 2nd place will receive $50, and 3rd place will receive $25.
  • Lunar Client will be forced on the entire Practice server.
  • At the end of the season, the 1st place players in each competitive ladder will be awarded a custom made "Practice Season 1 Winner" Lunar Client cape.
  • Players will be able to select the map they want to play on when dueling others.
  • New arenas will be added.
  • Basic rank and above will be able to start HCF team fights.
  • Epic rank and above will be able to start Tournaments.
  • Voice chat will be enabled in team fights.

Bunkers

  • All statistics will be reset.
  • New maps will be added.
  • Lunar Client will be forced on Bunkers.
  • At the end of the season, the person with the most wins will be awarded a custom made "Bunkers Season 1 Winner" Lunar Client cape.
  • Players with Epic rank and above can use /announce.
  • Voice chat will be enabled.

KitMap

  • All statistics will be reset.
  • At the end of the season, the person with the most kills will be awarded a custom made "KitMap Season 1 Winner" Lunar Client cape.
  • Lunar Client will be forced on KitMap.
  • New koths and a new spawn will be added.
  • Daily challenges have been added (/challenges).
  • The Kit will be the same as on Squads (Protection 2, Sharpness 2).
  • Building is enabled starting at (350,350)
  • Claiming is enabled starting at (500,500)
  • DTR will be lost in the warzone (for those who want to Practice their HCF skills!).

Vaults (coming soon...)

  • New game to be added to Lunar (an all-time favorite from Kohi).
  • Lunar Client will be forced on Vaults.
  • At the end of the season, the person with the most wins will be awarded a custom made "Vaults Season 1 Winner" Lunar Client cape.
  • Games will consist of 4 teams of 20.
  • Players will spawn in their own corner of the map. In order to build a an emerald "Vault," players must mine and sell their ores in the shop. The more ores that are sold, the more emerald blocks each vault has. After 10 minutes, walls separating each team will drop and items will spawn in the middle of the map.
  • In order to eliminate a team, players must destroy the other teams' vaults. The last team standing wins!
  • Players with Epic rank and above can use /announce.
  • Voice chat will be enabled.
