Squads Map 2

Squads Map 2 SOTW Time

  • Saturday, May 25th, at ~3PM ET / ~12PM PT

Squads Map 2 Information

  • 15 Man Squads
  • Bard & Archer enabled (each limited to one per Squad)
  • DTC (Destroy the Core) has been implemented
  • Rods now do damage to players
  • Force invites will be enabled on the second day of the map. Each faction will have 7 force invites. These will reset before and after Citadel.
  • Terrain generated roads
  • Percentage & Time based KOTHS
  • 1 minute cooldown before using a life
  • Gopples are disabled
  • Pearling through string is enabled

Squads Map 2 Kit, Potions, and Deathbans

  • Protection: 2
  • Sharpness: 2
  • Power: 4

Any enchantments and potions not stated are set at default levels.

  • Slowness: 1:07 Only
  • Poison: 0:33 Only
  • Lesser Invisibility: Enabled (Only works passed warzone; will go away if you get PvP tagged)
  • Weakness: Disabled
  • Invisibility: Disabled
  • Strength: Disabled
  • Instant Damage: Disabled
  • Regeneration: Disabled

Deathbans

  • Default: 3 hours
  • Basic: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Master: 2 hours
  • Legend: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Epic: 1 hour
  • King: 45 minutes

Closing Points

  • We will be having a base building competition. The winning faction will receive ranks for the next map along with a special Lunar Client cape. We will select a winner one day before EOTW. Once your factions' base is complete, please submit a ticket under the "HCF" category on https://www.lunar.gg/tickets.
  • The winners of this Maps' EOTW will be receiving a special Lunar Client cape.
  • Since DDoS is a big issue, no official rule regarding revives, rollbacks, etc will be made. These will be dealt with by a case-by-case basis by Head Staff.

As usual, we're going to be monitoring the forums the next few days to look out for suggestions, feedback, and questions regarding Squads Map 2. If you have any issues, please submit a ticket at https://lunar.gg/tickets and a staff member will gladly help you.

We hope you guys have a fun map!

~The Lunar Management Team
