Miner Class

Last edited by Jordan

Summary:

Miner is a class that gives you special effects as you mine X amount of diamonds (see below).You must be in full iron armor to activate this class once activated you will recieve constant haste 2, night vision 1 and invisibilty below y 20. In miner class you can use a pickaxe with efficiency 5 to quickly mine stone. However, after you mine 125 diamond ore you will receive haste 4 and will be able to use efficiency 4 instead of 5.

Diamond Ore Upgrades:

50 Diamonds = Haste 3 Upgrade
100 Diamonds = Speed 1 Upgrade
125 Diamonds = Haste 4 Upgrade
250 Diamonds = Fire Resistance Upgrade
400 Diamonds = Speed 2 Upgrade
600 Diamonds = Regeneration 1 Upgrade
1000 Diamonds = Saturation 1 Upgrade
